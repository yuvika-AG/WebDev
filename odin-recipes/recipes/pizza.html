  • Pizza Crust Ingredients
    • 2 cups maida (all-purpose flour)
    • 1 tsp yeast
    • 1 tsp sugar
    • ½ tsp salt
    • 2 tbsp oil
    • ¾ cup warm water
    recipe
    1. Take warm water in a bowl, add sugar and yeast, mix and keep aside for 10 minutes until foamy.
    2. In another bowl, add flour and salt.
    3. Add the yeast mixture and oil to the flour and knead into a soft dough.
    4. Cover the dough and let it rest for 1 hour until it doubles in size.
    5. Punch the dough to remove air and roll it into a round pizza base.
    6. Place the base on a greased baking tray or pan.
    7. Spread pizza sauce evenly on the base.
    8. Add grated cheese over the sauce.
    9. Add vegetables of your choice and sprinkle oregano and chilli flakes.
    10. Bake in a preheated oven at 200°C for 12–15 minutesOR cook on a covered pan on low flame for 10–12 minutes.
    11. Remove when the base is crisp and cheese is melted.
    12. Slice and serve hot.
